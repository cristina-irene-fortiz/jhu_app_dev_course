Our Menu

Chicken

Indulge in the comforting flavors of the South with our tender chicken, marinated in a rich honey mustard blend and kissed with a touch of zesty lemon. Infused with fresh thyme and rosemary, this dish delivers a perfect balance of sweet and savory.

Beef

Our Angus beef steak is a carnivore's dream, featuring thick, juicy slices perfectly seared to lock in flavor. Each cut is infused with a fragrant marinade of roasted garlic, fresh rosemary, and a splash of full-bodied red wine.

Sushi

Experience a mythical fusion of flavors with our Dragon Roll Sushi. Delicate slices of raw tuna embrace creamy, tangy cream cheese, all wrapped in perfectly seasoned rice and crispy seaweed.
